Who are we?

There is something about parenthood that gives us
a sense of history and a deeply rooted desire to
send on into the next generation the great things we
have discovered about life.

And part of that is the desire to instill in our children
the love of science, of learning and particularly the
love of nature.

What’s up with the name?

technology everyday, and a good example of this is
the LCD monitor. LCD monitors have several benefits
over the old chunky computer monitors that most
users are familiar with. Old computer monitors, take

heat, drain a ton of energy, and most often have low
picture quality and resolution.
